Lotto Probability: What the Numbers Really Mean

Lotto is most enjoyable when expectations are mathematically grounded. This guide explains odds, independent events, and practical usage habits.

1. Jackpot odds: 1 in 8,145,060

For a 6/45 format, the number of combinations is 45C6.

Formula: (45×44×43×42×41×40)/(6×5×4×3×2×1) = 8,145,060

Every combination has the same probability, including sequences that look "too simple."

2. Independent events

Each draw is independent. Previous outcomes do not make a number "due." This is where gambler's fallacy commonly appears.

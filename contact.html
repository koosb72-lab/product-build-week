Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Why are lotto results different every time?

A. The generator is randomized by design, so each draw is independent. No hidden weighting is applied to specific numbers.

Q. Are uploaded photos stored in the animal face test?

A. No. Analysis is performed in-browser with on-device processing, and uploaded images are not stored on our server.

Q. I think an ad is disruptive.

A. We keep ad placement minimal. If a specific ad placement blocks usability, send details and we will review it.

Q. Can I suggest a new tool idea?

A. Yes. Send your idea by email with context and expected user value.
